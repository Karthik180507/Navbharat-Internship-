About NAVBHARAT INTERNSHIPS

Connecting Talent with Opportunity

NAVBHARAT INTERNSHIPS was founded in 2020 with a mission to bridge the gap between India's talented students and the country's most innovative companies. We recognized that while there are many talented students seeking valuable work experience, and many companies looking for fresh talent, the connection between them was often inefficient and opaque.

Our platform revolutionizes the internship process by providing a transparent, efficient, and fair system that benefits both students and companies. We've helped over 10,000 students find meaningful internships and assisted hundreds of companies in discovering their next generation of talent.

Our Mission

To create a seamless, transparent platform that empowers students to find the perfect internship opportunities while enabling companies to discover and connect with the best emerging talent across India.

Our Vision

To become India's most trusted internship platform, recognized for our commitment to fairness, transparency, and innovation in connecting students with meaningful career opportunities.
