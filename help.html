Help Center

How do I register as a student?

To register as a student, click on the "Student Registration" link in the navigation menu. Fill out the required information including your personal details, educational background, and skills. Once submitted, your profile will be created and visible to companies.

How does the matching process work?

Our system matches students with companies based on several factors including your skills, CGPA, location preferences, and the company's requirements. Companies can search and filter student profiles to find the best matches for their internship positions.

How can I check my application status?

You can check your application status by clicking on the "Check Status" link in the navigation menu after logging in. This will show you which companies have viewed your profile, shortlisted you, or made offers.

What happens after a company selects me?

When a company selects you for an internship, you'll receive a notification. Your profile will then become invisible to other companies to prevent duplicate selections. You can then communicate with the company through our platform to finalize the internship details.

Can I update my profile after registration?

Yes, you can update your profile at any time by visiting your profile page. However, some changes (like CGPA or major skills) may require verification if you've already been shortlisted by companies.
